I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Technology Management and Innovation at the New York University Tandon School of Engineering, working under the supervision of Prof. Oded Nov.

My research interests are in human-computer interaction (HCI) and information visualization. I conduct empirical studies to understand how data visualizations influence perception, decision-making, and self-reflection across diverse contexts, including journalism, bias mitigation, and personal data.
